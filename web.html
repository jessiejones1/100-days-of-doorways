doors you say?

Doorways. An odd one, don’t worry I know. This was a job completed out of my own interest. A door is a very personalised piece of architecture which is often overlooked. The style of the door, to the handle, to the knocker, even to the letter box. Security door or none? Doorbell or knocker? Lock box or spare key under the mat? The fascinating part; is that it’s mostly all subconscious decisions. A decision made and forgotten about which is then changed or fixed up over time: through different inhabitants.

A door is something that is viewed everyday (well maybe not during Covid Season) but never noticed. It is the first thing guests see, even before they see you. It is the only thing the Posty sees of you and it is always featured in the Uber Eats photos of proof that your chicken snitty made it to the right place.

This archive has been made for your pleasure, something of an appreciation of the Richmond area and the local personalities. Even my own door features so maybe you’ll learn a little bit more of me.
